Focus

Built for markets where information moves first.

Quantitative market research

We study pricing behavior, liquidity conditions, and event resolution patterns across prediction venues to identify repeatable edges and better execution frameworks.

Signal and data infrastructure

We build pipelines that convert fragmented public information into structured datasets suitable for fast analysis, backtesting, and live monitoring.

Information discovery systems

We are developing tools that help surface relevant developments earlier, reduce manual search cost, and make high-noise domains more tractable for technical operators.

Approach

Technical, disciplined, and infrastructure-first.

Our work sits at the intersection of market microstructure, applied machine learning, and information retrieval. The goal is not generic dashboards. The goal is a tighter research loop: collect cleaner data, evaluate hypotheses faster, and act with clearer probabilistic judgment.

Laplaxton is being built with a preference for simple, auditable systems and low operational overhead. The public site is intentionally minimal. The core work is the research stack behind it.
